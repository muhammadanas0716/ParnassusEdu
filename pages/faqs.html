Frequently Asked Questions

What is AstranAI?

AstranAI is an educational platform designed to supercharge your learning experience with personalized tools. From discussing past paper questions to creating quizzes and providing detailed performance analytics, AstranAI acts as your personal and perfect tutor.

How does AstranAI personalize my learning experience?

AstranAI uses intelligent algorithms to tailor content based on your progress and learning style. It analyzes your strengths and weaknesses to provide customized quizzes, detailed performance insights, and targeted study materials.

Is AstranAI free to use?

AstranAI offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes limited access to features, while the premium plan provides unlimited access to all the advanced tools and personalized support.

What subjects does AstranAI cover?

AstranAI covers a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and various other subjects. The platform continuously updates and expands its content to include more subjects and topics.

How can I get started with AstranAI?

Getting started with AstranAI is easy. Simply sign up on our website to create an account. Once registered, you can explore the free features or choose to upgrade to a premium plan for full access.

How does the quiz creation feature work?

The quiz creation feature allows you to generate custom quizzes based on specific topics or areas you want to focus on. You can set the difficulty level, number of questions, and types of questions to create a personalized quiz tailored to your study needs.

Can I track my progress on AstranAI?

Yes, AstranAI provides detailed performance analytics that help you track your progress. You can view your scores, identify areas of improvement, and receive insights on how to enhance your learning.
